The Pickle Pledge helps people change their lives by learning to see the opportunity in every adversity and by replacing learned helplessness with a spirit of initiative and self empowerment.

The Pickle Challenge for Charity helps you promote a more positive, pleasant and productive workplace culture. Use this app to record your complaints when you don’t have a physical quarter to deposit into the Pickle Challenge for Charity pickle jar.

This app uses the honor system and is absolutely anonymous – no one will ever know how many taps you recorded and whether or not you made a donation (or the amount of your donation if you do) to the designated charity.
